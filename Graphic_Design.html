Graphic Design

Graphic Design is the art of technology, it is also known as Visual Communication or Communication Design depending on the skill set. Graphic Designer will use many different methods to create and combine style, symbols, words, personality, imagination, and images to create a visual representation of ideas and messages across the world. Graphic Design is commonly used in company logos and branding, advertisements, publications, website graphics and elements and more you see an image somewhere it comes from a graphic designer. Composition is one of the most important structures of graphic design, specifically when using pre-existing materials or miscellaneous origins. Visual arts include works on websites and movies a lot of time and in which are mostly visual in nature using whatsoever from outdated media, to cinematography or digital art.
